When will my next payment be due?

After your initial instalment today, your next payment will be due in 28 days. Don’t forget, you can customise this to suit your schedule.

Is Openpay safe & secure?

Yes, protecting your information is very important to us. Openpay is a Level 1 PCI DSS Compliant Provider. This means that all sensitive information is encrypted and we maintain stringent physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to protect your information. View the Openpay privacy policy here.

Will I be charged interest?

Openpay does not charge any interest. Ever.

Are there fees?

There are no establishment or payment fees. If you miss a payment, a late fee of £7.50 will apply. To avoid this, contact Openpay to reschedule your payment date.

What if I want to return my order?

All refunds will be processed through us as normal, and Openpay will cancel payments and refund what is owed. Read the full terms and conditions here.
